Our Graph Project - Antioxidents

For our project we wanted to know which food held the highest value in antioxident. So, we did some research on Tuvalabs and found a lab that showed a graph of the highest value antioxidents. We found out that Sumac actually held the highest value of antioxident, with a level of 312. Some may think that spices have more value, which they do have a lot of value, but not as much, although being ranked #2 out of all of the food labeled.
